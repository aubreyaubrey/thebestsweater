> Pencil on Paper / 12 x 12"

##### LAVA FIELDS FOREVER

---

Run from what’s comfortable. 

---

Forget safety. 

---

Live where you fear to live. 

---

Destroy your reputation. 

---

Be notorious. 

---

I have tried prudent planning

long enough. 

---

From now on I’ll be mad.

---

*- Rumi*
